Vakte — Prayer Times, Qibla Compass & Prayer Guide

Accurate prayer times for your location, adhan notifications when prayer time begins, a precise qibla compass, and a step-by-step guide to performing the prayer. Everything runs on your device: no account, no server, no data collection.

NO ADS — EVER.

FEATURES
• Location-based times: six times from Fajr to Isha, calculated on-device (Turkish Diyanet method); works offline
• Live countdown to the next prayer; a colored progress bar for the current period (makruh times marked)
• Adhan notifications: hear the adhan when prayer time begins, or choose the standard notification sound; optional early reminder
• Qibla compass: true-north accurate direction with haptic confirmation when aligned
• This Hour: a relevant Quranic verse for each prayer period — Arabic, transliteration and meaning
• How to Pray: a rak'ah-by-rak'ah guide for every prayer
• Prayer surahs and supplications with Arabic, transliteration and meaning
• The 32 Obligations, the 99 Names of Allah, and the life of Prophet Muhammad (pbuh)
• Browse any date from the calendar
• Turkish, Arabic and English interface; light/dark theme

PRIVACY
Your data stays on your device. Location is used only on-device to calculate times and qibla; nothing is sent to any server, and no data is collected.
